Relevance of Projects at VIT

Engineering Design & Innovation (EDI)

EDI projects at VIT encourage students to apply engineering fundamentals to solve real-world problems. They emphasize innovation, teamwork, and creativity while providing hands-on exposure to design and prototyping.

  • Fosters innovation-driven mindset
  • Hands-on learning with prototypes
  • Promotes interdisciplinary collaboration

Design Thinking (DT)

DT projects focus on human-centric design and problem-solving frameworks. Students learn to empathize with users, define problems, ideate, prototype, and test solutions systematically.

  • Encourages empathy-based innovation
  • Improves problem-solving skills
  • Structured methodology for real-world issues

Course Projects

Course projects are embedded in the curriculum and allow students to apply theoretical concepts in practical scenarios. They strengthen academic learning while preparing students for research and industry-level projects.

  • Application of classroom learning
  • Boosts technical and research skills
  • Enhances employability and career readiness
